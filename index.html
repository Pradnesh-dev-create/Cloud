About Fit Life

Welcome to Fit Life Studio — where fitness meets motivation! Founded by certified fitness trainer Alex Parker, our mission is to help you reach your health goals through personalized workouts, positive energy, and a supportive community.

Whether you’re new to fitness or a seasoned athlete, Fit Life provides a range of programs to suit your needs — from strength training to yoga, and everything in between.
